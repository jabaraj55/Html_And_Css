JAWA PERAK

Jawa Perak is a cruiser bike available at a starting price of Rs. 2,11,368 in India. It is available in 2 variants and 1 colour with top variant price starting from Rs. 2,13,187. The Jawa Perak is powered by 334cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 30.2 bhp and a torque of 32.74 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Jawa Perak comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Perak bike weighs 185 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 13.2 liters.

JAWA 42

Jawa 42 is a cruiser bike available at a starting price of Rs. 1,74,573 in India. It is available in 5 variants and 10 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 1,97,142. The Jawa 42 is powered by 294.72cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 26.95 bhp and a torque of 26.84 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, Jawa 42 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This 42 bike weighs 182 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 13.2 liters.

JAWA CLASSIC

The Jawa Jawa or Jawa 300 is a modern classic roadster with design which is near identical to the original Jawa two-stroke 250 cc motorcycles from the 1960s and '70s, although the new Jawa comes with a four-stroke 300 cc engine. Even though it's not a performance-oriented motorcycle, the Jawa looks great, is built well, and has brilliant ride and handling, backed by the performance of an engine which doesn't really have too many negatives.
